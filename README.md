Fighting-Blind-Prototype
========================

A rapid game prototype about fighting in the dark. Made in three days.

Controls:
--------

	PLAYER ONE
	
		Move:	WASD
		Attack:	F or Z
		Block:	G or X
		Sneak:	S (down)
	
	PLAYER TWO
		
		Move:	Arrow Keys
		Attack:	< or K
		Block:	> or L
		Sneak:	Down Arrow
